Designer Apprenticeship

What would you say is your experience with designing for the web?

At Group Buddies, we focus on designing products for the other side of the screen, aiming for an intuitive and delightful experience for the users.

Our Design Apprenticeship will make you dig deeper into how the user perceives design and how to design solutions for their problems, instead of decorations.

Beginner

You don't have any experience in designing products or even websites, but you do want to follow that path and learn more about it. We can help you by recommending that you take Creators School Design course, which will take you to a whole new level.

Intermediate

You've got some experience designing for the web and even designed a couple of products, regardless of the outcome. You understand what User Experience means and you focus on designing for your users and not for your own subjectivity.

If this sounds like you, we believe this is a great opportunity for you to learn faster by solving real problems from real people.
